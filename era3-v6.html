Architectural Choice

Why JSON despite the tax?

1. Hard Contract: JSON provides a strict schema. We trade context space for zero-hallucination intent mapping.

2. Native Tokenization: LLMs are trained on massive codebases; they are native JSON speakers.

Why it fails

The LLM may still choose the correct tool, but the hardcoded bridge is rigid. It parses a fixed contract. One changed field is enough to crash the orchestration loop.
